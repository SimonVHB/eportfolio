Who am I?

A more comprehensive explanation

As mentioned earlier, I am Simon Vanhaverbeke, born on May 14, 1997 in Roeselare.

I had always had a passion for computers. Ever since I was little, I was sitting behind a PC. It started playing games, then I discovered the internet and eventually started to program myself. You could find everything online, how do you make website, how do you write programs, you name it. So that is how my passion for IT came about.

Besides IT, I also like organizing things for others. From the moment I started studying in Gent, I commited my free time to others. That is how I got into the home council of my hall of residence, where I was, among other things, treasurer. In addition to that, I also help with the organisation of the Student Kick-Off, a festival for students, by students. Here I have the function of Hardware Coordinator, which naturally falls back on my passion for IT.

I am also sporty, I have played volleyball for years, and therefore do not like to sit still. Besides being helpful and sporty I think I am someone who always creates atmosphere and I am a fast learner.
